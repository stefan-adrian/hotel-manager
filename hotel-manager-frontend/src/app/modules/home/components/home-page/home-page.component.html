Welcome to Maxim Hotel


The Maxim Hotel offers five star luxury accommodations in the center of Iasi Romania near Palas Mall. Thanks to the great location you can take advantage of all the city without long drives to arrive to most of the city most desirable locations. Being located at five minutes walk to Palas Mall you can go there shopping for everything you like. Also, near the hotel you can easily find many luxury restaurants and pubs, so you can easily enjoy exquisite dining and thrilling nightlife. At a ten minutes walk you can find one of the most desirable tourist location in Iasi The Palace of Culture.

Rooms Booking

Maxim Hotel features a number of different luxury rooms and suites types to best accommodate you. Some of the rooms type are King Room, Double Single Room and Double King Room. If you want a premium staying at our hotel you can choose one of our suites. Our most popular suites are the Junior Suite and Superior Suite. To all the rooms and suites you can come at 2 PM the day you made the booking and you must leave at 12 PM on the last booking day. Of course we offer you a way to book you're staying online or when you come to the hotel. We advise you to book online though to make sure the room you want is available and also to get the best possible price.
